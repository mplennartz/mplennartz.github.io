📋 BACKGROUND DOSSIER

Dr. Nico Rylan is a board-certified medical examiner with over 15 years of experience in forensic pathology. Born and raised in a family of medical professionals, Nico developed an early fascination with the intersection of medicine and justice.

After completing medical school with honors, Nico specialized in forensic medicine and worked for several years as a county medical examiner. It was during this time that the Bureau took notice of Nico's exceptional analytical skills and ability to remain calm under pressure when dealing with unusual medical cases.
