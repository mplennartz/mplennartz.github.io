📋 BACKGROUND DOSSIER

Donovan Alexander Cross was born in London, England and holds dual citizenship with the United States. By age 43, he has established himself across three distinct spheres: as a billionaire tech mogul, a government special agent, and an elite K9 trainer—earning him the alias "Dogfather."

His aristocratic upbringing provided him with refined social skills and an instinct for command. His transition from an executive role to a more direct command position suggests a man who grew dissatisfied with boardrooms and chose to lead from the front. His drive as a Leader is defined by a deep sense of responsibility—he makes decisions and lives with the outcomes—but this same quality creates isolation, a distance imposed by rank that makes genuine closeness with those under his command difficult.

His expertise in animal training and handling, combined with his leadership and communication abilities, makes him a unique asset within CURSED's operational structure.
