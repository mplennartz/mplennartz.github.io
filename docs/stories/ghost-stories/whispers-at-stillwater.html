📋 INITIAL REPORT

The Bureau received an urgent request from the Cason family regarding unusual phenomena occurring at Stillwater Estate, a historic property located in Fiddler's Green, West Virginia. The estate, originally constructed in 1693, has been in the Cason family for generations.

The case came to our attention through a personal connection - Investigator 89 received a direct appeal from family members reporting escalating supernatural disturbances. Given the investigator's intimate knowledge of the property and family history, this presented a unique opportunity for insider investigation while also requiring careful attention to potential emotional complications.

PROPERTY INFORMATION:
Name: Stillwater Estate
Location: Fiddler's Green, West Virginia (formerly Cross Hallow)
Construction Date: 1693
Original Builder: Elias Par (revivalist preacher)
Current Owners: William and Anne Cason
Historical Note: Built on site of 1700s revival baptism ground

The team arrived at the estate after a road trip through the misty backroads of West Virginia. The property looms dramatically over a glacial lake, its ancient timbers and flickering gas lamps creating an atmosphere of gothic foreboding. As autumn mists rise from the water, the estate's long corridors carry an unsettling silence.

👁️ WITNESS TESTIMONIES

Anne Cason, Property Owner:
"A few nights ago, the lights started flickering throughout the house. We had an electrician come out to check all the wiring - he said everything was perfectly fine, code compliant, no issues at all. But the flickering continues. It started as just occasional flickers, but now it happens multiple times per night. The noises started off as just a few knocks - like a broom falling or something simple. Now they've grown much louder, and we're hearing voices coming through the walls."
Recorded: March 15, 1999, 14:30
William Cason, Property Owner:
"This house has a history. It was originally built by Elias Par, a revivalist preacher. There were rumors about him - that he 'didn't always speak in the language of God,' if you know what I mean. The estate was constructed on an old 1700s revival site. Stories were told of drowned folk being baptized and coming up 'not right'. I never put much stock in old stories, but with everything happening now..."
Recorded: March 15, 1999, 14:45
Esther Vale, Estate Maid:
"I'm the only one left. All the other servants have fled. Edward, the groundskeeper, he ran off without even taking his shoes. When Mr. Cason tracked him down, Edward wouldn't open his door. He spoke through it saying 'he wants nothing, ever again, to do with that Cason house.' He's left town entirely now. I've been hearing voices too - someone praying. It sounds like the voice is coming from under the floorboards."
Recorded: March 15, 1999, 15:00

📚 HISTORICAL RESEARCH

The property's documented history reveals multiple concerning elements that may contribute to current supernatural activity:

Original Site History: The estate was constructed on the location of a 1700s revival baptism site. Historical accounts describe disturbing incidents where individuals who were "drowned" during baptismal ceremonies and subsequently revived exhibited altered behavior - described in period documents as coming up "not right".

Elias Par, Original Builder: The revivalist preacher who commissioned the original structure had a controversial reputation. Contemporary accounts suggest he engaged in practices outside mainstream religious traditions, with specific reference to him "not always speaking in the language of God" - potential indication of glossolalia, channeling, or other spiritualist practices.

Name Change Mystery: The area was originally known as Cross Hallow, but the name was changed to Fiddler's Green around World War I. William Cason specifically mentioned that any historical documents referencing "Cross Hallow" should be preserved, suggesting awareness of suppressed local history.

INVESTIGATION PRIORITY:
Primary Objective: Locate and examine ancient scroll in attic library
Secondary Objective: Map estate layout, identify sealed root cellar location
Tertiary Objective: Locate and document tree carving with spiral/weeping eye symbol
Evidence Collection: Document all scratches, architectural anomalies, and acoustic phenomena

🏚️ ESTATE LAYOUT & POINTS OF INTEREST

The investigation team is preparing to conduct a thorough examination of the estate's interior and grounds. Initial reconnaissance has identified several areas of particular interest:

  • Attic Library: Contains historical documents and the mysterious scroll written in unknown language
  • Nursery: Site of unexplained scratches on walls
  • Parlor: Location of persistent mysterious draft near fireplace
  • Servants' Quarters: Bones discovered beneath floorboards
  • Sealed Root Cellar: Location uncertain, mentioned by family matriarch
  • Estate Grounds: Tree with carved spiral and weeping eye symbol
  • Glacial Lake: Adjacent to property, possible connection to baptism site history

🔬 INTERIOR INVESTIGATION

With the preliminary interviews complete, the team split into groups to systematically examine the estate's interior. Investigator 89 and Investigator 51 proceeded to the study on the upper floor, while Investigators 66, 07, and 13 continued interviewing the housekeeper, Esther Vale.

The Study Discovery: In the study, Investigator 89 began examining the extensive collection of historical documents. While searching through a roll-top desk, the investigator discovered a hidden compartment. Inside were yellowed pages describing something called "angelic transformation" with repeated references to an entity known as "He Who Waits Beneath."

EVIDENCE LOG - HIDDEN DOCUMENTS:
Location: Concealed compartment in study roll-top desk
Content: Ritual instructions for "Transfiguration by Tongue"
Purpose: Fusion of spirit and flesh; notes suggest reversal process possible
Age Analysis: Paper dated to 1700-1800s (confirmed by Investigator 51)
Handwriting: Male, possibly matching Elias Par's writing style
Entity Reference: "He Who Waits Beneath" - origin unclear

Investigator 89 immediately consulted with William Cason regarding potential verification of the document's authorship. William confirmed the existence of a painting with a handwritten note from Elias Par on the reverse - this could serve as a handwriting comparison sample.

When questioned further about Elias Par's history, William recalled additional disturbing details: Par suffered a tragedy involving the death of his daughter. Following her death, rumors circulated that he engaged in ritualistic acts with her corpse.

🚼 THE NURSERY DISCOVERY

Investigator 89 and Investigator 51 then proceeded to the nursery to examine the scratches mentioned during the initial interview. What they found was far more disturbing than expected.

While inspecting the wallpaper, Investigator 51 noticed an unusual texture beneath one section. Peeling it back revealed deep scratches carved through multiple layers of wallpaper, plaster, and even penetrating into old wood paneling beneath.

EVIDENCE LOG - NURSERY SCRATCHES:
Location: Behind wallpaper on nursery wall
Characteristics: Deep gouges penetrating multiple layers of material
Height: Approximately child-height from floor
Force Analysis: Depth inconsistent with child's natural strength
Tool Assessment: No evidence of implement use - appears to be hand-created
Additional Finding: Similar scratches discovered beneath antique crib
Pattern History: Scratches have reappeared multiple times despite wall remodeling

When Investigator 89 suggested demolishing a section of wall to examine what lay behind the scratches, William Cason strongly objected, explaining they had recently remodeled the room specifically to hide the recurring scratches. William confirmed that no new damage had appeared since Investigator 89 (as a child) had moved out of the nursery.

Critical Connection: The scratches only appeared when a member of the Cason bloodline occupied the room. This suggests a direct spiritual connection to the family lineage.

🔨 BREACHING THE ROOT CELLAR

Meanwhile, Investigators 66, 07, and 13 continued interviewing Esther Vale, who provided crucial information: the bones discovered years ago were found under the pantry floor, directly above the sealed root cellar.

Investigator 66 immediately sought Anne Cason's permission to access the sealed cellar. As discussions continued, an unsettling change occurred throughout the estate - an oppressive silence fell over the house, followed by the distinct sound of a door creaking open somewhere in the building.

As Anne and Investigator 66 stood in the pantry, they heard it: three distinct knocks emanating from beneath the floor.

With reluctant approval from Anne Cason, the team began the process of breaching the pantry floor. After careful work, they successfully removed the linoleum and floorboards, revealing the entrance to the long-sealed root cellar.

🕯️ THE BINDING CHAMBER

The entire investigation team descended into the root cellar. What they discovered was a chamber that had clearly served a dark ritualistic purpose.

ROOT CELLAR FINDINGS:
Atmosphere: Dark, cold, oppressive presence
Wall Markings: Sigils and occult symbols carved into stone surfaces
Binding Circle: Iron nails hammered in ring formation around central dark stone
Religious Defacement: Christian symbols deliberately corrupted and inverted
Spectral Residue: Strong paranormal energy signature detected
Document Fragment: Partially burned vellum on stone surface

Investigator 89 recognized that the nail pattern and symbols mirrored the diagrams found in the study's hidden documents. The arrangement constituted a binding ritual designed to contain a spiritual entity.

On the central stone, Investigator 89 found a scrap of burned vellum. Though mostly destroyed, the phrase "Transfiguration by Tongue" was clearly visible - matching the documents from the study.

As the team examined the chamber, the atmosphere became increasingly hostile. The floor began to vibrate with an almost organic pulsing. A frigid wind rushed through the sealed cellar, ripping the vellum fragment from Investigator 89's hand. A voice began to emanate from the walls themselves - broken segments of prayer, simultaneously pleading and warning, though the words remained unintelligible.

📖 RECONSTRUCTING THE RITUAL

By cross-referencing the fragments from the study documents with the symbols in the root cellar, Investigator 89 successfully reconstructed the nature of the spiritual threat and the method required for banishment.

RITUAL REQUIREMENTS FOR BANISHMENT:
Component 1: Defaced Bible (from the binding circle)
Component 2: Blood of a Stillwater heir (Cason family descendant)
Component 3: True name of the entity spoken aloud
Process: Corrupted prayer must be reversed and spoken backwards

The most critical missing element was the entity's "true name." The documents referenced "He Who Waits Beneath," but William Cason recalled this phrase was actually a mistranslation of an older Native American term.

Investigator 89 consulted with Anne Cason, whose expertise in anthropology might provide the answer. Anne indicated that while she didn't know the translation, William possessed several binders of Elias Par's original sermons that might contain the information.

In the study, William provided the sermon collection. Investigator 89 worked rapidly through the documents, decoding fragments of Par's invocations. The true name emerged: SULEMAZOTH - circled repeatedly throughout the sermons with obsessive frequency.

👤 THE ENTITY IDENTIFIED

Through analysis of the complete ritual documents and historical context, Investigator 89 pieced together the horrifying truth about the entity haunting Stillwater Estate.

ENTITY PROFILE: SULEMAZOTH
Origin: Hybrid fusion of human soul and primordial land-spirit
Human Component: Reverend Elias Par (19th century revivalist preacher)
Spirit Component: Ancient land-spirit predating colonial settlement
Creation Method: "Transfiguration by Tongue" ritual performed by Par
Motivation: Par's grief over daughter's death led to attempt at divine communion
Binding Location: Fused to soil beneath Stillwater Estate (formerly Cross Hollow)
Current State: Dormant for generations, now awakening
Manifestation Goal: Seeking vessel to rise again and resume "preaching to the living"

The ritual Par performed fused his mortal soul with the primordial entity, binding their merged essence to the land itself. The entity had remained largely dormant for generations but was now experiencing an awakening pulse, likely triggered by the presence of Investigator 89 - a direct blood descendant of the family that had owned the estate for centuries.

⚔️ THE BANISHMENT

With all components identified, the team returned to the root cellar to perform the banishment ritual. Investigator 89 led the ceremony, using the blood of a Cason heir (provided willingly by Investigator 89), the defaced Bible from the binding circle, and speaking the true name while reversing the corrupted prayer.

The ritual did not proceed without resistance. As the reversed prayer was spoken, Sulemazoth manifested as a towering apparition - a horrific fusion of human form and something far more ancient. The entity lashed out physically, attempting to prevent its banishment.

BANISHMENT ENGAGEMENT REPORT:
Resistance Level: EXTREME - Physical manifestation achieved
Team Casualties: Minor injuries sustained during spiritual attack
Ritual Success Factors: Correct components, proper invocation, blood connection
Outcome: SUCCESSFUL BANISHMENT - Entity dispersed
Verification: Immediate cessation of all paranormal activity
Property Status: No residual spiritual presence detected

Despite Sulemazoth's violent resistance and the physical harm inflicted on team members, the banishment ritual was successfully completed. The entity's manifestation dissolved, and the oppressive atmosphere that had permeated Stillwater Estate immediately lifted.

✅ CASE RESOLUTION

Following the successful banishment of Sulemazoth, the investigation team conducted thorough follow-up monitoring of Stillwater Estate. No further paranormal activity has been detected.

FINAL CASE STATUS: CLOSED - SUCCESSFULLY RESOLVED
THREAT LEVEL: NEUTRALIZED
Entity Status: Sulemazoth banished from material plane
Property Status: Safe for habitation
Family Status: Cason family has safely reoccupied the estate

Case Closure Date: March 16, 1999

The Cason family has safely returned to full residence at Stillwater Estate. The historical documents related to Elias Par have been catalogued and secured for future reference. The root cellar has been resealed, though all occult symbols have been ceremonially cleansed.

Investigator 89 has been cleared for future field work with no indication of lasting spiritual attachment. Medical treatment was provided for all team members who sustained minor injuries during the final confrontation.

Historical Note: This case represents a successful resolution of a multi-generational haunting involving direct spiritual fusion between human and primordial entity. The Transfiguration by Tongue ritual has been documented for future reference should similar cases emerge.

The Bureau extends gratitude to the Cason family for their cooperation and to the entire investigation team for their professionalism in handling a case with significant personal connections and high spiritual danger.
