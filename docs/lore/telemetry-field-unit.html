📖 OVERVIEW

Bureau Field Telemetry Unit (Class T) is the working designation for a compact handheld instrument observed on a desk inside Argencorp's concealed NeXstep reactor control suite. Field descriptors compared its form factor to legacy science-fiction survey tools; internal shorthand: "tricorder-class." The same device class was found at Investigator 89's feet after the NeXstep Displacement Event, implying it either traveled with the team or was seeded at the arrival point.
