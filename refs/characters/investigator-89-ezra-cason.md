## Background Dossier
In the family homes, estates, and hallowed halls of education of New England's academic elite, the name Cason resonates with a legacy of erudition and a penchant for delving into the occult's enigmatic depths. Ezra Cason, an erudite gentleman of just 30 years of age, is the scion of the illustrious Cason family, esteemed for their generations-long dedication to scholarly pursuits in the realms of the occult and esoteric knowledge.

Ezra's childhood started in the town of Fiddler's Green, WV where the family home estate is—though his parents were often going to the New England area as they were well respected academicians. His upbringing was unlike that of his peers, steeped in an atmosphere where the mystique of the arcane permeated every corner of the ancestral manor. From an early age, he found himself entranced by the forbidden allure of ancient tomes, cryptic manuscripts, and esoteric artifacts that adorned the family's opulent library. He often lingered near the adults during the various and many parties his family would host—listening to the stories, theories, and tales of the occult. Many close family friends of the Casons—the who's who of New England's academic elite—attended these parties: the MacInneses, Harrisons, Watkins, Femmers, and many others.

Though Ezra grew up upper class, the generational Cason estate stood as a bastion of knowledge, a place where learning and studying were given the highest priority.

Guided by his family's rich heritage, Ezra embarked on a scholarly journey. He pursued his education at Brown University, receiving a Bachelor of Science degree in Computers and Cryptography. He immersed himself in his studies. Ezra went on to receive his PhD in Computer Science from Miskatonic University in Arkham, Massachusetts.

Ezra's academic pursuits, however, were not confined to the walls of academia alone. Fueled by an insatiable thirst for forbidden knowledge, he embarked on wondrous expeditions to distant lands, from the shadowed catacombs beneath European cities to remote corners of Asia where his sharp research skills were incredibly useful. His travels brought him face-to-face with arcane texts, hidden relics, ciphers, and much more, helping broaden his understanding of the world around him.

Now, in his early thirties and standing as a beacon of great scholarship, Ezra is a distinguished adjunct professor at both Miskatonic and Brown University, as well as a highly sought-after guest lecturer on researching and hacking techniques. Now fully recognized within the esteemed lineage of the Cason family, and his mind a repository of immense wisdom, Ezra was recently working with a small group of paranormal investigators in the Miskatonic area; however, that crew was short-lived. This is when his old Brown College dorm roommate, and long-time friend Bertram "Bertie" Wooster, reached out to Ezra. Bertie and Ezra have worked on many projects in the past. Bertie recently joined up with a small start-up investigation company, C.U.R.S.E.D., and knowing Ezra would have some useful skills for this new group, reached out and asked Ezra to join. Ezra jumped at this opportunity as he was wanting to get back to his core knowledge of computers and cryptography.
