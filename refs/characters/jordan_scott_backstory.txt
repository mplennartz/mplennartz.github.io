Background Story

Jordan Scott was a seasoned undercover cop with a talent for reading people and making them believe exactly what she wanted them to. She had worked dangerous cases before, slipping into roles with ease, but nothing could have prepared her for what happened that night. She and her partner, Ethan Cole, were sent to infiltrate a warehouse where a major drug deal was supposed to go down. But the moment they stepped inside, everything unraveled.

What should have been a routine bust turned into a waking nightmare—strange technology, unnatural phenomena, and a sense of wrongness that defied explanation. In the chaos, Ethan vanished, and Jordan barely escaped with her life. The official report listed Ethan as dead, but Jordan couldn’t shake the feeling that there was more to the story, that he might still be alive, somehow.

After the incident, Jordan was plagued by relentless nightmares and a need for answers that her department wanted to ignore. When her obsession made her a liability, they pushed her out, leaving her adrift with nothing but questions and a growing distrust of the system she once served. Then CURSED came knocking. A consulting firm specializing in the unusual, the unexplained, and the dangerous, they offered her a second chance—not just to chase the truth, but to finally uncover what really happened that night.

Jordan is closer than ever to the answers she’s been chasing. But she’s learned the hard way—getting too close can cost you everything.
