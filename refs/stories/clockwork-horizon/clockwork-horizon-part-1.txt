Back at HQ, Katja enters and lays a folder in front of us – saying she has an high priority assignment.
Looking at the notes – it is for Argencorp again – in one of their facilities on the west coast.
Seems a bunch of Argencorp’s quantum computer components have gone missing.  
Argencorp’s “NeXstep” facility in Redmond, WA seems to be the primary site of interest.
There is also a note of interest in the dossier, a few years ago, it states Argencorp acquired a small miniature nuclear reactor to help supplement their energy needs.
Giving the crew’s past history with Argencorp, Ezra asks if this facility also has AI tech running the building – Katja confirms it does not.
Katja not having much more info for us, we make our way to the Thurgood Marshall airport to catch our flight to SeaTac airport.
After arriving at SeaTac, we make our way to the Argencorp NeXstep facility.
As we arrive at the site, we are approached by Marcus Hale – head of security on shift.
Hale tells us that we have to put all our weapons and electronic devices in security lockers at the guard shack – no weapons or electronic devices are allowed.
Even though it was requested – we all opt to sneak a handgun in and do so successfully.
Also, with some distraction from Jordan and Donovan – Ezra is able to sneak in his laptop.
Set to go, Hale escorts us into the building.
Wanting to get a longer leash from Hale – so we can more freely move about – we opt to enter an advanced test to persuade Hale to allow us more freedom to move about. We have a TN13 and a Threshold 12.
With some solid rolls, we are able to convince Hale that we are capable and know enough of Argencorp’s protocols to allow us a little more wiggle room and granted us access to the restricted labs on level 3.
